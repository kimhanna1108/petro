The advertised value of 7 cents per litre is based on the following:

Each time you use your linked RBC Card to purchase any grade of gasoline, or diesel, at a Petro-Canada location, you will save three cents ($0.03) per litre at the time of the transaction.

Each time you use your linked RBC Card to pay for Petro-Points purchases at a Petro-Canada location, you will earn a bonus of twenty percent (20%) more Petro-Points than you would usually earn. This means that for every litre of fuel purchased, a linked RBC card can earn 1.2 cents in redeemable Petro-Points value (based on a redemption value of 1,000 Petro-Points = $1). Petro-Points are not awarded on tobacco products, vaping products, gift cards, transit tickets and taxes on non-petroleum purchases. Only certain RBC cards earn Avion points/RBC Rewards points.

Only certain RBC cards earn Avion points on net purchases. This means that for every litre of fuel purchased, a linked RBC Card that earns Avion Rewards on net purchases can earn up to 2.8 cents of redeemable Avion Rewards value, calculated based on the average fuel cost of $1.65/litre and the average travel redemption value across all types of available travel redemptions options in accordance with the Avion Rewards Terms and Conditions at avionrewards.com/terms-and-conditions.
