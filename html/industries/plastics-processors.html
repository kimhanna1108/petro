As a plastics manufacturer, you're always looking for new ways to improve productivity and lower operating costs without compromising product quality. 

Petro-Canada offers a complete line of lubricants that can keep up with tough operating conditions, including extreme heat and pressure. 

They're produced from one of the purest base oils in the world — 99.9% pure. So you can count on consistent quality in a variety of plastic processing applications, including: 

  • injection moulding
  • extrusion
  • blow moulding
  • thermoforming
  • lamination
