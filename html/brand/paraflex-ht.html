PARAFLEX HT process oils are carefully controlled blends of paraffinic base oils produced by removing undesirable polar and aromatic compounds.

Composed of saturated hydrocarbons, PARAFLEX HT process oils are crystal clear and have low toxicity.

  • Outstanding oxidative and thermal stability
  • Crystal clear appearance
  • Very low toxicity
  • Very low aromatic levels
  • Superior separation from water

PARAFLEX products are used as manufacturing aids or as raw materials in manufacturing a wide range of chemicals, elastomers, and other specialty products.

Derived from paraffinic stocks, their carefully controlled hydrocarbon content means PARAFLEX HT Fluids can be used in a number of industrial products:

  • Lubricants
  • Chemicals
  • Rubber and plastics
  • Leather
  • Adhesives
  • Polishes
  • Plate glass / glass wool
