DuraDrive HD products are specially formulated for commercial and heavy-duty (HD) transmission systems where a severe duty and/or extended drain interval fluid is specified. DuraDrive HD Synthetic 668 is a licensed and approved factory fill fluid for on-highway and vocational Allison Transmission transmissions that need a TES 668® approved fluid. This is fully back serviceable to those transmissions previously filled with TES 295® and TES 468™. Other products include DuraDrive HD Synthetic Blend ATF and DuraDrive HD Long Drain ATF.

  • Outstanding resistance to oxidative and thermal breakdown
  • Exceptional low / high temperature fluidity
  • Excellent anti-wear protection
  • Stable friction properties
  • Compatible with all transmission seal materials
