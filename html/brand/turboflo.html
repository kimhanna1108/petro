TURBOFLO handles the extreme conditions facing your steam, hydraulic and gas turbines. In fact, TURBOFLO fluids outperform many leading competitive lubricants in various tests.

Gas, steam and hydraulic turbines operate at high temperature and pressure extremes. TURBOFLO fluids reduce overall maintenance costs by extending intervals between oil top-up and complete change-outs.
