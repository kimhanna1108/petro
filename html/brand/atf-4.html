Formulated using 100% PURITY™ VHVI Synthetic Base Fluids and a unique additive system, Petro-Canada ATF+4®‡ contributes to the overall performance of the transmission by delivering optimized shift efficiency, improved oxidation and shear stability and extended drain intervals over ATF+3®# fluids.

It has been extensively tested and approved by FCA US LLC under their Material Specification MS-9602. 

ATF+4®‡ is the recommended top-up and refill automatic transmission fluid for Chrysler, Dodge, and Jeep vehicles with automatic transmissions where ATF+4®‡, and ATF+3®# is recommended.

  • Provides enhanced oxidation stability, high shear stability and optimized friction properties for Chrysler, Jeep and Dodge transmissions
  • Superior wear protection and anti-shudder durability
  • Improved low temperature flow
  • Provides extended drain interval over ATF+3®#
  • Ensures smoother cold weather operation
