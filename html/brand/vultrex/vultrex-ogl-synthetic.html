Advanced “Solution Provider” products containing synthetic fluids to provide excellent protection for handling the harshest operating conditions that can be encountered – from severe winter cold to extreme summer heat and from very wet to dusty environments.

  • Outstanding protection against wear and surface damage on gears and slides
  • Solvent-free formulation reduces lubricant consumption and fire risk
  • Adhesive properties resist wash-off from exposed surfaces under wet or snowy conditions
  • Contains a darkener for enhanced visibility on open gears, to prevent over-lubrication
