For pneumatic rock drills and similar mining equipment used in underground mining, formulated for mist-free operating in underground mining.

  • A more productive, environmentally sensitive and cleaner alternative to oil lubrication
  • Cost-effective: it can be applied using existing oil lubricators
  • Runs cooler than oil lubrication

  • Formulated for the lubrication of air operated rock drills and similar mining equipment used in underground mining
  • Operating temperature range is -12°C to 135°C (10°F to 275°F)
