SENTRON stationary gas engine oils are high performance, long-life oils used for stationary gas engines and compressors in a wide variety of applications with a key focus on gas compression and power generation.


Engine type examples include:

  • Turbocharged or naturally aspirated
  • Stoichiometric or lean/ultra-lean burn engines
  • Older diesel converted engine design
