THE EVR PRODUCT LINE

DRIVELINE

  • 'Wet' Applications: Fluid solutions designed for optimal viscosity, efficient cooling, and compatibility with e-motor and gear-set materials

  • 'Dry' Applications: Lubricants designed to protect reduction gear sets and provide efficient energy conversion for high RPM e-motors

ELECTRIC MOTOR GREASE

  • Polyurea-based grease specifically designed for the requirements of EV electric motor bearings

THERMAL MANAGEMENT

  • Direct Cooling: Immersive lubricant solutions designed to optimize safety, material protection, and heat transfer and temperature uniformity

  • Indirect Cooling: Solutions for thermal efficiency and performance, optimized for electrical conductivity and dielectric performance
