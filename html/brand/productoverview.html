Petro-Canada Lubricants produces over 350 advanced lubricants, specialty fluids and greases. Our products perform better and last longer because they start with 99.9% pure base oils.


We leverage the exceptional characteristics of our base oils along with 45+ years of research, development and formulating expertise. Then we add a unique combination of additives to create lubricants with advanced performance – leading to:


  • Reduced equipment downtime
  • Cost savings through lower maintenance costs
  • Increased productivity
