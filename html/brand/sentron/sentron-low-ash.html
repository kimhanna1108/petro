Petro-Canada offers an extensive line of SENTRON Low Ash Products. SENTRON Stationary Gas engine oils are premium performance, long-life engine oils for stationary gas engines and compressors in a wide variety of applications.

  • Extended oil life and excellent control of engine deposits
  • Exceptional overall engine protection for maximum reliability and savings
  • Advanced piston cleanliness
  • Verified and approved by many OEMs

Suitable for 4-stroke cycle gas engines requiring a Low Ash product with either traditional or non-traditional gas sources

SENTRON LD 8000 (0.52% wt ash)

SENTRON LD 8000 provides revolutionary performance of up to 300%[1] longer drain intervals over the leading global conventional competitor. SENTRON LD 8000 contains a specialized additive mix that provides greater TBN retention to help neutralize acids that can attack liner surfaces. SENTRON LD 8000 helps prevent deposits from ash and worn metal parts – deposits that could otherwise result in higher oil consumption.


Applications

A premium Low Ash SAE 40 lubricant, suitable for use in 4–stroke engines operating on natural pipeline gas, pre-treated sewage/biogas and selective pre-treated process gases.

SENTRON LD 5000 (0.60% wt ash)

SENTRON LD 5000 provides excellent performance for up to 200%[2] longer drain intervals and exceptional overall engine protection for maximum reliability and savings. SENTRON LD 5000 helps provide reduced valve recession and controls wear of piston rings, cylinder liners and bearings for prolonged engine life vs. competitive oils.


Applications

Petro-Canada recommends SENTRON LD 5000 for use in Caterpillar, GE (Jenbacher/Waukesha) and many turbocharged or naturally aspirated, stoichiometric or lean burn engines that require a Low Ash natural gas engine oil.

SENTRON LD 3000 (0.47% wt ash)

SENTRON LD 3000 helps provide a high level of performance in high output turbocharged engines that prefer a Low Ash oil. It provides excellent control of engine deposits, even when there is a need for added high temperature deposit control.


Applications

Recommended for most 4-stroke and some 2-stroke gas engines. It is suitable for use in engines fitted with catalytic converters. It is also suitable for severe service applications including high output, turbocharged engines that encounter high exhaust gas temperatures. The result can be significantly less valve sticking for reduced backfiring in these severe applications.

SENTRON CG 40 PLUS (0.52% wt ash)

SENTRON CG 40 PLUS provides excellent control of deposits and other issues caused by high halogen gas levels produced by landfills. Unlike conventional landfill / biogas / sewer gas engine oils, SENTRON CG 40 PLUS is specifically designed to address current and future engines running in this severe contaminated gas application where higher BMEP engines are used.


Applications

SENTRON CG 40 PLUS is a uniquely designed Low Ash formula for 4-stroke gas engines running in severe service landfill gas operations.
