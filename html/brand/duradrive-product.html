Offers true multi-vehicle performance, outstanding wear protection, and exceptional fluid life.

DuraDrive MV Synthetic ATF provides the frictional properties, wear protection and viscometrics needed by most major North American, Asian, and European automatic transmissions.

  • Excellent resistance to oxidative and thermal breakdown
  • Exceptional low / high temperature fluidity
  • Outstanding anti-wear protection
  • Provides industry leading anti-shudder durability (ASD) and frictional stability, exceeding the performance of many genuine OEM fluids
  • Compatible with all transmission seal materials
  • Consistent shift feel and transmission protection over a long fluid life
