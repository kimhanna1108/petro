A premium heavy duty diesel engine oil formulated with a focus on better durability and engine protection.


Formulated to API CJ-4 category standards, DURON EXTRA helps you get the most out of every engine, stay productive, reduce costs and improve your reliability, no matter how tough the operating conditions.

  • Durability: Formulated with strong resistance to oxidation and designed with high shear stability to help extend drain intervals[1] and productivity
  • Tough in all Temperatures: Formulated to resist the high temperatures of sustained heavy duty use, and to provide critical protection in all types of weather
  • Enhanced Protection: Provides long term protection against engine wear and the build-up of engine deposits and harmful sludge even under extreme conditions.

개요

DURON EXTRA 15W-40 is a premium heavy duty diesel engine oil formulated to deliver:

  • Extra durability
  • Engine protection
  • Performance

Approved by API for API SERVICE CJ-4 category standards on or off-road. It is designed to give added protection no matter how tough the conditions.


특징 & 장점

  • Extra Tough Performance: Formulated specifically to ensure extra protection in cold start up conditions. As well as performing at high temperatures of sustained heavy duty use.
  • Soot Protection: Designed to give extra protection in the build-up of soot and sludge with resistance to the formation of acids which can lead to engine damage.
  • Extra Durability and Drain Intervals: Extra performance for engines to help extend drain intervals[2]and productivity.
