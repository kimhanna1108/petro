Formulated for use within environmentally sensitive areas as well as for use in industrial manufacturing plants.

  • Contains no metal-based additives, which minimizes occurrence of waste water contamination by metals
  • Non-toxic and very low odour contributes to a cleaner, safer and more pleasant working environment
  • Can be recycled and reclaimed
  • Inherently biodegradable hydraulic oils that biodegrade between 20% to 60% within a 28-day period
  • Improved oxidation and thermal stability compared to competitive vegetable oil based products and conventional hydraulic oils
  • Provides anti-wear, rust and corrosion protection

For use in woodlands equipment, drilling, marine, construction and mining operations as well as industrial manufacturing plants.

적용 분야

  • Formulated for use within environmentally sensitive areas as well as for use in industrial manufacturing plants
  • Can be used in hydraulic pumps with silver bearings such as Lucas pumps because they will not displace the silver in these bearings, as they do not contain zinc-based anti-wear additives.
  • NSF H2 listed (no allowable food contact)
