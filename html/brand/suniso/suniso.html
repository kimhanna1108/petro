WORLD-LEADING REFRIGERATION LUBRICANTS

The Suniso® product range has evolved to match the requirements of modern refrigeration systems and changes in lubricating base oil technology.

Specially formulated to provide exceptional stability, maximize service life and deliver trouble-free performance, there's a reason why Suniso has been the world's leading refrigeration lubricants brand for more than 90 years.

Suniso GS

Premium quality, highly-refined mineral refrigeration lubricants designed to lubricate refrigeration and air-conditioning compressors using CFC and HCFC refrigerants.

Suniso SL

High-performance, synthetic refrigeration lubricants formulated for optimal performance of compressors using using HFC and HFC/HFO refrigerant blends.

Suniso 4HT

A hydrotreated refrigeration lubricant designed to lubricate commercial and industrial refrigeration compressors using ammonia (R-717) refrigerant.

Suniso 4SA

A highly refined naphthenic refrigeration lubricant designed to lubricate refrigeration compressors using ammonia (R-717) refrigerant.
