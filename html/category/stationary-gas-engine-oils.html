The severe operating conditions common to natural gas fuelled engines used in gas compression, power generation and cogen/CHP demand a Stationary Gas Engine Oil that delivers performance and extended oil life while helping you maintain excellent control of engine deposits.

Petro-Canada Lubricants SENTRON™ Stationary Gas Engine Oils are a family of premium performance, long-life natural gas engine oils specially formulated to lubricate natural gas engines and power generation equipment.
