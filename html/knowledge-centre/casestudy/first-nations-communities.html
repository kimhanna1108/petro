Partnering With First Nations Communities

It is very important for Petro-Canada Lubricants, an HF Sinclair brand, to partner with many of the First Nations communities across Canada as we support the mining sector and our mining customers with lubricants, greases, expertise and solutions.


To show some of the depth of our involvement with those communities, we’ve gathered testimonials. These testimonials help explain our partnerships together and how they benefit our customers to accomplish their goals.


We are very proud to be members of the Canadian Council for Aboriginal Business. These partnerships are extremely important to our business and to our customers and we are honored to be able to continue our membership with this community.

"We worked as partners to demonstrate to IAMGOLD the value and the positive impact of our program for the local community and the Côté Gold Mine site. We made official our supply agreement with IAMGOLD in 2022 and continue supply with them today. We are proud to continue our partnership and support for our customer. "

Tim Harvey,
CEO, Mattagami AKI,
Mattagami First Nation



"Naicatchewenin First Nation is very honoured to be in partnership with Petro-Canada Lubricants, and to continue delivering excellence in service at New Gold in Rainy River."

Dean Bethune,
General Partner of Anokiigamig Construction LP,
Naicatchewenin First Nation
