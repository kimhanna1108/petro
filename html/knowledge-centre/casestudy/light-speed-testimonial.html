About Light Speed Logistics


Based in Alberta, Canada, Light Speed Logistics offers trucking, shipping, hauling, freight and logistics services in the highly regulated and time-sensitive perishable food sector. The business’ large team driver driven fleet with up to 90K lb loads means they can provide expedited services to most destinations in Canada and the USA within 48 hours, with drivers working in shifts and vehicles being on the road 24/7.

The Challenge

Founded in 2000, Light Speed Logistics has a fleet of 350+ Navistar and Mack power units and 800+ refrigerated trailers along with a fleet of tri-axle trailers for intra-Canada freight. With most equipment less than three years old and fully monitored through online data technology, Light Speed Logistics has one of the newest fleets in the industry.


Light Speed Logistics needed quality products and exceptional technical expertise to guarantee the effective and efficient maintenance of their new and existing equipment.


Always looking for opportunities to take advantage of the latest technologies and developments in engine oil technology, the company wanted to know if there was a better-quality product out there that would provide them with the opportunity to extend oil drain intervals, which would in turn reduce maintenance costs and increase time on the road.


The Solution

Light Speed Logistics turned to their lubricant supplier, Jepson Petroleum, a Petro-Canada Lubricants distributor based locally in Calgary, Alberta for expert insight about a lubricant that would help meet the operational and equipment needs of the forward-thinking fleet.


Petro-Canada Lubricants technical experts recommended a field trial for API FA-4 DURON Advanced 5W-30 to demonstrate proof of performance for Light Speed Logistics’ newer Cummins X-15 and Mack MP-8 engines.


Supported throughout the trial by technical experts, a robust used oil analysis program was implemented enabling Light Speed Logistics to benefit from real-time insight into the performance of their fleet and engine oil.

The Results

After a one-year field trial, DURON Advanced 5W-30 has demonstrated easier cold start in the harsh Alberta winter and enhanced engine protection through its outstanding oxidation stability and exceptional BN (Base Number) retention.


If Light Speed Logistics upgraded the entire fleet to DURON Advanced 5W-30 and extended all drain intervals from 80,000 km to 130,000 km, predicted total cost savings of CAN $1.26 million per year could be achieved from:

  • C$1 million per year cost savings for reduced downtime
  • C$260,000 per year saving on reduced cost of oil, filter and labor
