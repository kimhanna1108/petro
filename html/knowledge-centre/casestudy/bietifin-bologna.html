BIETIFIN 소개

BIETIFIN은 바이오가스 분야에서 기술 지원 서비스를 제공하는 이탈리아의 선도 기업입니다. 본사는 볼로냐에 위치해 있으며, 국가 비트 재배자 협회(Consorzio Nazionale Bieticoltori)의 규제를 받습니다. 이 회사는 비트 재배자들과 협력하며 비트 산업에 기술적 및 노조 관련 지원을 제공합니다. BIETIFIN은 200개 이상의 플랜트를 운영하며, 그중 20개 이상은 직접 소유하고 있습니다. 광범위한 책임을 감당하기 위해, BIETIFIN은 자체 연구개발 센터를 운영하여, 직접 소유한 플랜트들의 성능 향상을 위해 매일 노력하고 있습니다.


효율성을 운영의 핵심 가치로 삼고 있는 BIETIFIN에게 있어, **작은 성과 향상(marginal gains)**도 매우 중요합니다. 그 중에서도 엔진 오일은 개선 여지가 큰 핵심 영역 중 하나이며, 특히 오일 교환 주기(oil drain interval)를 늘리는 것이 주요한 개선 포인트입니다.

The Challenge

Provide a stationary gas engine oil to extend drain intervals at Bietifin’s key locations

 

With efficiency at the heart of Bietifin’s operation, finding marginal gains is crucial for the organization. Engine oil is one key area where this is possible, especially when it comes to oil drain intervals.


San Giuseppe Agroenergia is one of the plants owned by Bietifin and in early 2016, its Jenbacher Type 3 ECOMAX 6 – JK720 – 635 kW engine required an oil change on average every 1,690 hours. Biotre Energy,another of Bietifin’s plants, used a Jenbacher Type 3 ECOMAX 10 – JN897 – 1 MW and needed an oil change every 2,000 hours or less.


The frequency of downtime associated with oil changes can impact an organization’s efficiency and its ability to provide power to crucial services.


Bietifin sought out a premium stationary gas engine oil to extend oil drain intervals and reduce equipment downtime, supporting the company’s commitment to improving efficiency.

The Solution

A premium-performance stationary gas engine oil that can stand up to severe operating conditions


Petro-Canada Lubricants SENTRON™ gas engine oils are high-performance, long-life engine oils used for stationary gas engines in a wide variety of applications, with a key focus on power generation and gas compression.


Bietifin switched to Petro-Canada Lubricants SENTRON™ CG 40, a medium-ash oil designed specifically for 4-stroke cycle gas engines running in severe service.


Bietifin’s plants provide power for industries that are crucial to their local communities. As the power output required for these industries is high, it’s important that output is reliable.


SENTRON™ CG 40’s exceptional oil life and anti-wear protection make it well-suited to the severe conditions experienced at Bietifin’s plants and research centre. Following initial testing of the product at San Giuseppe Agroenergia, Bietifin extended its use to more of its locations, including its research and development centre.

The Results
  • Oil drain intervals increased by over 50%
  • Engine downtime reduced
  • Cost savings achieved
  • Engine efficiency improved

Switching to Petro-Canada Lubricants SENTRON™ CG 40 enabled Bietifin to improve its operating efficiency at a number of its plants and locations.


Two of the company’s biggest plants, San Giuseppe Agroenergia and Biotre Energy, both extended drain intervals to beyond 3,000 hours, an increase of over 50%.


Such gains were beyond the expectation of operators at Bietifin’s plants, which has led the company to explore the use of the product throughout its 200-plant portfolio, as they operate under the same severe conditions.


SENTRON™ CG 40 also demonstrated improved performance compared to the existing oil, namely its anti-wear protection and exceptional oil life.


The switch to SENTRON™ premium stationary gas engine oil has enabled significant gains in the extension of oil drain intervals. This has supported Bietifin in improving its operating efficiency and allowed it to provide an enhanced service to vital local industries.
