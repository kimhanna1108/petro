On-Highway Class 8 Fleet Vehicle

Protect your fleet. Drive your productivity.

Fleets that know lubricants matter choose Petro-Canada Lubricants. Our complete heavy-duty lubricant product line has a global reputation for performance levels so high, it makes fleets more reliable and businesses more profitable. 

DURON™ is our most durable heavy-duty diesel engine oil product line ever - ready to take you further between oil drains* and help you use less fuel across your whole fleet.
